A single-page website that changes with the time of day. Morning has one personality. Afternoon another. Evening another. Night is different from all of them.

It's minimal — almost nothing on the page except what the time of day brings. Subtle color shifts, changes in typography weight, different ambient textures. Nothing dramatic. Just enough to make you feel the passage of time.

The latest version adds sunset-aware color data based on your actual geographic location. So dusk in Connecticut looks different from dusk in California. Because why shouldn't a website know what time it is where you are?

It's quiet. It's atmospheric. It's a little bit magic.

**Tech:** Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS, SunCalc library
